About Us

Radharaman Ayurveda Medical College Research Hospital

The group is well known for innovation and academic excellence. RGI combines best of both the worlds. Here the technology, individuality & independence of the west are blended in right proportion with the culture, traditions & sensitivity of the east. The success story of Radharaman Group of Institutes begins in the year 2003 when the Typasya Education Society launched its first Medical College i.e.Radharaman Ayurveda Medical College Research Hospital

Chairman's Message

Welcome to Radharaman Ayurved Medical College Research Hospital, Bhopal. Established in 2003 under the Tapasya Shiksha Samiti and led by former professors and alumni of MANIT, our institution aims to prepare the younger generation for the future through Ayurveda. Our mission is to develop each student's potential, helping them become top clinicians, academics, researchers, and entrepreneurs who serve as brand ambassadors for Ayurveda. We focus on the all-round development of our students, motivating them to become responsible citizens and high-standard medical professionals for the welfare of the people.

R.R.Saxena, Chairman RGI
